The Process

Three Steps to a Successful Sale

Every home is different, and every seller has unique goals. Kevin's personalized approach ensures your strategy is built around you.

1

Define Your Goals

This can be a very complex question. The simple answer is always, make a profit. But selling a home is more than that.

  • Are you relocating?
  • Selling a rental?
  • How quickly do you need to sell?
  • Getting a bigger home?
  • Downsizing?

By answering these questions, you can identify the WHY behind selling. Your goals will determine your strategy.

2

Create a Strategy

Strategies are not one size fits all. What you determined in step one will shape your selling strategy.

  • Home improvements
  • Clean & declutter
  • Searching for new home
  • Finding financing for sell & buy
  • Pricing (high, low & expected)
  • When to list / how to list / how to show
  • Ideal closing / move out date / 1031 tax exchange
3

Develop a Marketing Strategy

Even in a hot real estate market you can make more money with a good marketing strategy.

  • Reaching out to prospective buyers before listing
  • Scheduling agent-only open house
  • Listing on Thursday for weekend showings
  • Promoting open houses in advance
  • Professional photography
  • Advertising on Facebook, Google, LinkedIn, Instagram, Zillow, Realtor.com, MLS, and TikTok
