My name is Kevin Freel and I'm a real estate agent with CENTURY 21 LIST with Beggins in the Tampa Bay Area. I've been in the real estate industry since 1985. Originally from Indiana, my family moved to Dunedin in 1970.   I started selling properties in Dunedin, Clearwater & Palm Harbor.  I moved to South Tampa in 1996 and eventually expanded the areas I cover. I'm proud to help families sell and buy throughout the Tampa, The Beaches, St Pete, Clearwater, Lutz, and Odessa areas. I'm passionate about providing an amazing client experience and I thrive on helping my clients buy, sell, invest, and even lease out their properties. My expertise is guiding you through the entire process, from start to finish. I provide unique strategies tailored to your needs, ensuring your real estate dreams become a reality. When I'm not helping clients, my hobbies include working out, biking, traveling, and trying new foods. I'm always looking for ways to stay active and explore the Tampa Bay Area. I'm proud to be an experienced real estate advisor for over 40 years now. It's a privilege to have served over 1,000 families....
