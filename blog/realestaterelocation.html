From High School Friends to Dream Home: A Tale of Serendipity in Tampa Bay

Watch the video →

Real Estate Relocation: From NYC to Tampa

When the 80s called and gave me a personal referral, little did I know it would lead to reconnecting with an old high school friend and helping a couple find their perfect home in Tampa Bay. It all started with a text from Janice Peppe, a past client and dear friend from my high school days. Her close friends from New York were in the market for a home in the Tampa Bay area and were cash buyers. Excitement bubbled within me as I dialed the number Janice provided.

A Broad Home Search Across the Greater Tampa Area

James and Dawn were the stereotypical eager homebuyers. They had a clear vision of what they wanted—a 3-bedroom, 2-bathroom abode with a garage, nestled in a vibrant 55+ community. They were excited, and they wanted to move quickly. 

They had searched the internet, finding area’s around Tampa Bay they liked. The search was broad across three counties: South St. Pete, Clearwater, Dunedin, Palm Harbor, Tampa, and Pasco County. Their budget? Up to $450,000.  Each location held its own promise, a unique blend of community, nature, and lifestyle waiting to be discovered.

House Hunting with Tampa’s Best Real Estate Agent

Staying with friends in town, my buyers had allocated three consecutive days to travel all over Tampa's diverse neighborhoods, we walked through 17 homes in just three days. This speed tour was not just about viewing properties; it was about feeling the pulse of each neighborhood, imagining life within those walls and streets, and weighing the subtle nuances that differentiate one home from another. After all, an hour north of Tampa and you are in horse country. Head West and you are living in a cute beach community. You can stay in the city and find great homes, or move into a sprawling suburban subdivision. It all depends on you, or in this case, my buyers.

Finding THE Tampa Home

Then, like a stroke of fate, we stumbled upon the one—a breathtaking home in Pasco County's Bexley, nestled within the Del Webb Community. This 55+ haven boasted amenities galore—a pool, clubhouse, gym, dog run, and a plethora of activities to indulge in. But what truly stole James and Dawn's hearts was the solitude offered by this particular property—no neighbors in the front or rear, just serene tranquility.

Built in 2019, the home stood as a testament to modern comfort and style. Thanks to its recent construction, the home inspection was a breeze, solidifying our confidence in the purchase. And come the end of March, we sealed the deal.

Moving In and Calling It Home

Seeing the joy on James and Dawn's faces as they stepped into their new abode was the ultimate reward. Their excitement was palpable, and I couldn't have been happier for them. It's moments like these that remind me why I love what I do.

And as for Janice, my high school friend turned forever client, she still resides in the very home I helped her purchase back in 1997—a testament to the lasting relationships forged through the journey of real estate.
