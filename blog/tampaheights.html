Luxury Living in Tampa Heights: Three Stunning New Townhomes

Watch the video →

Tampa Heights has become one of the most sought-after neighborhoods for homebuyers looking to blend city living with historic charm. Today, I’m excited to share three exceptional townhomes that exemplify modern luxury, prime location, and top-tier craftsmanship. But before we dive into the details, let me tell you how these properties came into my hands—it’s a story that highlights the power of connections and the personal relationships that make real estate more than just transactions.

A Chance Encounter That Led to an Incredible Opportunity

Real estate is often about being in the right place at the right time, and this journey started with an open house. A few years ago, I was hosting a property showing when I received a call from Texas. The caller was the daughter of a potential buyer who had found my listing and wanted her father to attend the open house in her place.

When he arrived, we realized we already knew each other—he was my doctor! What started as a simple viewing turned into a full-circle real estate experience. I helped his daughter purchase a home in South Tampa, then worked with his son to find a waterfront property. Eventually, my doctor-turned-client approached me to sell his investment property in Tampa Heights, which brings us to one of today’s featured homes: 1801 N Morgan St Unit #6.

1801 N Morgan St Unit #6 – Contemporary Townhome in Tampa Heights

A Masterpiece of Modern Luxury | $1.175M 

Located just steps from Downtown Tampa, this newly constructed townhome offers the perfect mix of style and convenience. Designed for urban city buyers, the exterior maintains a timeless aesthetic, while the interior is a showcase of modern sophistication.

Level One: A Flexible Entryway and Spacious Garage

As you enter, a welcoming foyer leads to a versatile flex room with a private full bath, ideal for use as a home office, guest suite, or media room. The spacious two-car garage provides ample parking and storage, a rare find in city living.

Level Two: Open-Concept Living with High-End Finishes

The heart of this home is on the second level, featuring a modern, chef-inspired kitchen equipped with:

  • Thermador appliances for top-tier performance

  • A large center island with quartz countertops for cooking and entertaining

  • A pot filler and abundant cabinetry for added convenience

  • A breakfast area overlooking the spacious family room

Two balconies on opposite ends provide outdoor spaces for fresh air and relaxation, while high ceilings create an airy, open feel.

Level Three: A Luxurious Primary Suite and Additional Bedrooms

The third floor is home to the primary suite, complete with:

  • Two separate closets for optimal organization

  • A spa-inspired en-suite bathroom with designer finishes

  • Luxury vinyl plank flooring throughout

This level also includes:

  • Two additional bedrooms with generous space

  • A hallway bath with modern touches

  • A large upstairs laundry room

  • A walk-in storage closet for extra convenience

Prime Location in Tampa Heights

This townhome sits in the heart of Tampa Heights, one of the most vibrant and desirable neighborhoods in the city. Just minutes away from:

  • Armature Works – A premier dining and entertainment hub

  • The Tampa Riverwalk – Scenic paths connecting parks, restaurants, and cultural venues

  • Historic Ybor City – A lively district filled with history, nightlife, and charm

  • Hyde Park Shopping Village – Upscale shopping and dining
