The Real Estate Market In Tampa Is Still Going Strong

Yes buyers are more cautious & the government raised the interest rate last week. However, mortgage rates have stayed pretty consistent for the last 90 days and inventory levels are at a 2 months supply. For the market to shift from a seller's market to a balanced market, we would need to see inventory climb to a 5 months supply! 

What does this mean to you? If you are thinking of selling and think you missed your opportunity, you haven’t!

There are still many buyers out there looking for a great property, but how you price your home is key to selling vs sitting.
