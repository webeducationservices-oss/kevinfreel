Beating the Market Trends

The real estate market has experienced its ups and downs this year. Despite a slight cooling in closed sales and an increase in inventory, my clients and I have consistently beaten the trends. The median sale price has remained relatively stable, and there has been a noticeable increase in high-end home sales. Here’s a quick overview of the market statistics:

  • Closed Sales: A slight decline of 3.0% compared to last year.

  • Median Sale Price: A stable $435,000, showing only a minor decrease of 0.8%.

  • Inventory: A significant increase of 61.5%, suggesting a shift towards a buyer's market.

  • Months Supply of Inventory: Increased to 3.3 months, up by 83.3%.

While the market has its challenges, my sellers and buyers are thriving because they work with me. My dedication to understanding market dynamics and providing personalized service has ensured that my clients achieve their real estate goals.
