A Heartfelt Thank You For Another Amazing Year

Watch the video →

As this year comes to a close, I want to pause and say thank you to the incredible clients, friends, and referral partners who made it such a remarkable one.

This year is especially meaningful for me as I celebrate 40 years of selling real estate in the Tampa Bay Area. This is a milestone that fills me with gratitude, pride, and excitement for what’s still ahead. Because of your trust and confidence, I had the privilege of closing approximately $15,000,000 in sales volume this year. That’s 29 homes bought or sold. This number represents far more than transactions. It represents relationships, shared goals, and dreams realized.

I’m deeply thankful to each of you who chose me to guide you through one of the most important decisions of your life. Your loyalty, referrals, and continued support are the foundation of my success.

As we head forward, I’m excited to share that I currently have beautiful homes on the market, with exceptional new listings coming soon, and I’m already hard at work planning for a strong and successful 2026. The Tampa Bay market continues to evolve, and I remain committed to delivering experience, integrity, and results at the highest level.

From the bottom of my heart… thank you for being part of my journey. I look forward to continuing to serve you and those you care about for many years to come.


Happy Holidays!
