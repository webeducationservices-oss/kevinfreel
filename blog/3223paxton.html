First Floor: Open Living, Flexible Bedroom, And Chef’s Kitchen

From the moment you step inside, 10 foot ceilings and abundant natural light create a bright, welcoming feel. The first floor is designed for both daily life and entertaining.

Flexible Bedroom Or Office With Full Bath

A main level bedroom with a closet and connecting full bath gives you options. It works well as:

  • A private guest suite

  • A dedicated home office

  • Space for older family members

  • A quiet study or hobby room

Having a full bath on this level makes it especially convenient for guests or anyone who prefers to avoid stairs.

Gourmet Kitchen And Open Great Room

The kitchen is built for real cooking and gathering. Features include:

  • Custom 42 inch shaker cabinets with soft close drawers
    Level 2 designer quartz countertops

  • Designer tile backsplash

  • Pendant lights over a large center island

  • Upgraded stainless steel appliance package with built in double ovens
    Pot filler for easy cooking

  • Extended walk in pantry with solid shelving

The kitchen opens seamlessly to a spacious great room with large windows and a floating credenza. This open layout keeps everyone connected, whether you are hosting friends for a game or enjoying a quiet night at home.

Flooring throughout the main living areas is luxury vinyl plank, which offers the look of wood with durability that stands up to pets, kids, and everyday traffic. Tile in the bathrooms keeps those spaces fresh and easy to clean. A mudroom with built-ins off the two car garage helps keep clutter in check.

Second Floor: Primary Retreat, Ensuite Bedrooms, And Laundry

Upstairs, the design continues to focus on comfort and function.

Three secondary bedrooms are arranged for privacy:

  • Two bedrooms share a Jack and Jill bath, ideal for kids or siblings

  • One bedroom features its own en suite bath, perfect for guests or a teen who wants extra space

The primary suite is large enough for a king size bed and additional furniture, creating a true retreat. Storage is a standout here, with two walk in closets:

  • Closet 1: 10' 7" x 15'
    Closet 2: 7' 9" x 9' 8"

The primary bath feels like a spa, with a standalone soaking tub, a large shower featuring both a rain shower and a regular shower head, and dual vanities with extra storage. It is designed to be a place where you can unwind at the end of the day.

A large laundry room is conveniently located upstairs, close to the bedrooms. No more carrying baskets up and down stairs. That small detail makes a big difference in day to day living.
