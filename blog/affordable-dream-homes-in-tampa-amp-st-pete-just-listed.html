In the heart of Tampa, the quest for an affordable, move-in-ready home within a reputable school district is more than just a desire—it's a dream for many families. 308 E Hanna Ave, Tampa, FL 33604: A Charming Seminole Heights Bungalow

Discover the allure of Seminole Heights with this enchanting renovated bungalow at 308 E Hanna Ave. Every detail has been meticulously updated to offer a blend of classic charm and modern convenience. The heart of this home is its bright kitchen, adorned with Quartz countertops, stainless steel appliances, and a unique "pot filler" for culinary enthusiasts. The home benefits from a new roof, A/C, and flooring, ensuring peace of mind for homeowners. Two contemporary bathrooms feature decorative tile, adding a touch of elegance. Abundant natural light, a spacious laundry room with a pet spa, and a fully fenced backyard with a large shed equipped with electricity and A/C are just a few highlights. With every major system updated, including the foundation, this home is a testament to quality and attention to detail.

Highlights:

  • Listed at $440,000

  • Open Floor Plan & Original Hardwood: A seamless blend of living spaces with refurbished hardwood floors throughout.

  • Gourmet Kitchen & Renovated Bathroom: Quartz countertops, new cabinets, and 2 completely renovated bathrooms.

  • Essential Updates: New roof, AC, water heater, and appliances, ensuring peace of mind for the new homeowners.

  • Outdoor Potential: An expansive backyard with shed, room for a pool, perfect for entertaining or creating your own oasis.

730 50th Ave N, Saint Petersburg, FL 33703: Comfort Meets Convenience

At 730 50th Ave N, you'll find a home that has been lovingly maintained by the same owner of 26 years, now ready to welcome its new owners. Positioned just miles from Downtown St. Petersburg, this gem offers an open floor plan, original hardwood floors, and a dream kitchen with granite countertops and cherry wood cabinets. Recent renovations include a fully updated bathroom and essential updates to the roof, AC, and water heater. The third bedroom, with its separate entrance, presents versatile possibilities for guests or rental opportunities. The expansive backyard offers limitless potential for outdoor gatherings or a future pool, creating an oasis of comfort and convenience.

Highlights:

  • Priced at $400,000

  • Open Floor Plan & Original Hardwood: A seamless blend of living spaces with refurbished hardwood floors throughout.

  • Gourmet Kitchen & Renovated Bathroom: Granite countertops, cherry wood cabinets, and a completely renovated bathroom as of October 2023.

316 E Adalee St, Tampa, FL 33603: Modern Elegance in Historic Tampa Heights

The historic charm of Tampa Heights is beautifully showcased at 316 E Adalee St, where modern design meets timeless elegance. A brand-new roof and complete renovations throughout make this 3-bedroom, 2-bath home truly move-in ready. Enjoy entertaining in the spacious backyard or take advantage of the convenience of gated parking with an EV charger. Inside, find woodgrain tile flooring, new carpeting in the bedrooms, and an updated kitchen with Shaker-style cabinets and granite countertops. Security is a priority, with a comprehensive camera system in place. This home's location in the vibrant Tampa Heights community puts you minutes away from local hotspots and downtown attractions, offering an ideal blend of historical charm and modern living.

Highlights:

  • Priced at $519,900

  • Contemporary Renovations: Brand new roof, modern designer touches, and a complete renovation from top to bottom.

  • Spacious & Secure Outdoor Space: Large fenced backyard with an 8x8 storage shed and gated parking area featuring an EV charger.

  • Luxury Finishes & Security: Woodgrain tile flooring, brand-new carpeting, updated kitchen, and a 4-camera security system for peace of mind.
