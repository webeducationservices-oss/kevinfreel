Top 19 Reasons Why Your Out Of Town Friends & Family Should Move To Tampa Bay

When you think of Florida, you think about the great weather. And yes, the weather is what initially attracts most new residents. But did you know about all the other great things that make Tampa Bay the place to be? Forget about the rest, and check out the top 19 reasons why your out-of-town friends & family should move to Tampa bay! (yeah, make sure to forward/share this one with them)

REASONS FOR EVERYONE

1.   Warmer climate, best winter weather (yeah, it’s still a great reason to check out Tampa)

2.   No State Tax - Florida has no state tax. A nice surprise for some new residents at tax time.

3.   Tampa International Airport, one of the best! Oh, and Clearwater/St. Pete Airport is about 15 mins away!

REASONS THAT FIT YOUR PERSONALITY

4.   For you foodies, Tampa has some of the Best Restaurants, Bern’s, Charlie’s, Ocean Prime, Bella Brava, Hawker’s Asia Street Fare, Olivia, Miguel’s, Yummy House, Meat Market, Mise En Place, Cena, Salt Rock Grill.

5.   If you are more of a beach bum, Tampa offers some of the top 10 Best Beaches in the world! Clearwater, Dunedin, St Pete, Treasure Island, Anna Maria, St Armand.

6.   Sports enthusiast? We are the City Of Championship Sports! Tampa Bay Lightning Hockey, Tampa Bay Buccaneers, Tampa Bay Rays Baseball, and MLS, Rowdies! We also host the Pineapple League throughout the greater Tampa Area. That’s 15 MLS teams here in Tampa for Spring Training. 

7.   Many great hospitals, Tampa General Hospital,  Top 10% in the country.

8.   Want some city living? Downtown St Pete,  The Arts, Views, Restaurants, Neighborhoods, Outdoor Activities.

EVEN MORE REASONS THAT FIT YOUR PERSONALITY

9.    For the party-goers - we’ve got Gasparilla Parade & Festivals within 30 minutes of the city almost every weekend!

10.   The outdoor man can take a short drive or walk to amazing State, City Parks, & Biking Trails, all free!

11.   If water is your thing, Tampa offers the best Boating & Fishing along the riverways, bay, and gulf!

12.   For the avid shopper, there is International Plaza, Hyde Park, St Armand Circle, many smaller boutiques.

13.   Families can take advantage of many excellent private, public schools & nationally ranking universities!

WHAT MORE COULD YOU WANT?

14.   1 Hour away from Theme Parks, Disney, Universal.

15.   Unique neighborhoods to fit every personality, and many 55+ Neighborhoods.

16.   2 new crystal lagoon developments for residents & visitors.  Staycation year-round.

17.   Best waterfront properties,  beaches, intracoastal, rivers, lakes & ponds.

18.   Some of the most beautiful golf courses.

AND THE BEST PART ABOUT MOVING TO TAMPA!

19. There is a house for everyone! Tampa has a unique variety of homes ranging from multi-million dollar estates, multi acre properties, bungalows, waterfront high-rise condos, neighborhood ranch homes, suburban neighborhoods, new construction, historic districts, and everything in-between. You name it; we’ve got it.
