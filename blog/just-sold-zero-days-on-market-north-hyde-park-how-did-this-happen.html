June 15, 2021 Just Sold Market Update South Tampa

Just Sold! Zero Days On Market! North Hyde Park! How Did This Happen?!

The short story is... property value, a hot market, and an experienced Realtor.

The long story is a little more interesting.

I know this is a crazy market, and Realtors have to get creative if they want to help their clients. So to sell a home in 0 days, I got creative. 

I hit the streets. Not your traditional mass-market postcard, but literally driving around looking at homes that met my client’s needs. Then I did something unexpected. I wrote personalized letters–not an email, not a postcard, but a simple letter. 

Simplicity is the key to this story. I simply explained my client’s needs and the opportunity to the homeowner. Personalized communication led to a phone call, a discussion about the seller’s wants and needs, and a cash offer within 24 hours. The offer was accepted, and the home closed 30 days later.

The point of this story–you need experience, creativity, and an authentic Realtor that can get you what you want.

If you want to sell your home AS-IS for a cash offer, now is the best time. We have builders ready to buy and rehab. The lack of land and the hot housing market is creating opportunities that were never available. In the past, you had to replace a roof, rehab baths, replace ACs, update kitchens, and “get the house ready.” Now we can list your home without these updates, sell quickly, and still get a great return.
