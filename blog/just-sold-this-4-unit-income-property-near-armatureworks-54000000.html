Every transaction comes with new opportunities for my buyers and sellers, and I’m always grateful to be apart of the homebuying experience. If you’re thinking of creating an AirBnB, a small home is a great start.  

Some Tips:

1)  Check out IKEA, Home Goods, Target & small boutiques for charming furnishings you can buy.

2)  Offer a journal, have your guests post where they’re from, what activities did they do, what they suggest guests visit, during their stay.

3)  Create a book with pamphlets for local activities, restaurant menus, address of activities to explore.

4)  The more amenities, the better.  Hot tub, wine frig, bicycles, free weights, prop frame for selfies, fire pit, grill, veggie/herb garden, board games, a few movies, corn hole,  & e-scooters!  A nice touch would be some fresh flowers, maybe a welcome message in a basket with a bottle of wine.  Coffee maker, some coffee & tea, are a must. 

Some Excellent AirBnB Properties:  

• Anything near a downtown 

• Beach house

• Duplex 

• Charming Bungalows

What Vacationers Want:

• Customers want walkability to cafes

• Close coffee houses

• Good outdoor pubs

• Nearby shopping

Vacationers are looking for convenience and attractions, so pay attention to the neighborhood.
