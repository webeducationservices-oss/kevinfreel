Finding the Perfect New Construction Home in Tampa: The Challenge and the Solution

New construction in Tampa is scarce, making the search feel overwhelming for buyers. With Tampa’s rapid growth, you are facing limited options for homes that meet the latest building codes and energy efficiency standards. Whether you’re a growing family or looking for a luxury upgrade, securing a home that’s built to last and adheres to the latest safety and energy regulations can be tough. Fortunately, we have some new homes coming on the market. Take a sneak peek at Starling Custom Homes and their newest property in South Tampa, blending modern design, high-end finishes, and the peace of mind that comes with new construction warranties.

Under Construction – Ready by Summer 2025

This stunning new home by Starling Custom Homes is currently under construction and scheduled for completion by June 2025. Photos in this listing represent a finished home by the builder, showcasing their signature luxury style and attention to detail. The 3,467 sq. ft. home features 4 spacious bedrooms, 3.5 bathrooms, an office, a bonus loft area, and a 3-car garage. Designed for both everyday living and entertaining, the open floor plan includes a large great room and a chef-style kitchen complete with a breakfast area and a massive center island. This exceptional Starling Custom Home is competitively priced at $1,399,000. With its high-end finishes, spacious design, and prime location, this home represents an incredible value.

Luxury Features for Modern Living

Starling Custom Homes takes pride in quality and craftsmanship. This home offers high-end construction with superior finishes that elevate your living experience. Some of the standout features include:

  • High-impact glass windows for safety and energy efficiency

  • Crown molding throughout for added elegance

  • GE stainless steel appliances to inspire your inner chef

  • 42" decorative wood cabinetry and quartz countertops for a sleek, modern kitchen

  • Wood flooring throughout to add warmth and style

  • An upstairs laundry room for ultimate convenience

Additionally, the covered lanai and backyard create the perfect setting for entertaining friends and family in the beautiful Tampa weather.

Peace of Mind with Comprehensive Warranties

When you invest in new construction, you want the assurance that your home is built to last. This Starling Custom Home comes with extensive warranties to give you peace of mind.

  • 1-year warranty on defects in workmanship and materials

  • 2-year warranty on mechanical systems, including plumbing, electrical, and HVAC

  • 10-year structural warranty to protect against major issues

These warranties, backed by a reliable third party, ensure that your home is protected for years to come. For more information, visit www.2-10.com.
