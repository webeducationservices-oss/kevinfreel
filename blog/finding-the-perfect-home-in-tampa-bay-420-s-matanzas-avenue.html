Watch the video →

Tampa Bay is a highly sought-after location for those looking to purchase a new home, but finding the perfect property in the $500,000+ price range can be challenging. However, 420 S Matanzas Avenue offers buyers a rare opportunity to own a stunning property that ticks all the right boxes.

The Ultimate Investment Opportunity

This property provides a wide range of possibilities for potential buyers. Build up to three new townhomes, use it as a family home, or rent it out as an Airbnb or lease property. With excellent schools nearby, including Mitchell, Wilson, and Plant High, this property is perfect for families or those looking to invest in a high-end rental property.

A Charming Cottage with Modern Amenities

The 1949 cottage boasts a charming and inviting design, complete with a pantry, ample counter space, and a bright garden window in the kitchen. The property offers a living room, family room, formal dining area, and a large laundry room with tons of built-in storage, making it the perfect home for families.

The outdoor screened deck is ideal for entertaining guests or simply relaxing and enjoying the beautiful surroundings. There's also a large storage shed on the property, providing ample storage space. The home also features a newer A/C system, which is approximately three years old, making it an energy-efficient and low-maintenance property.

Perfect Location for Easy Access to the Best of Tampa Bay

420 S Matanzas Avenue is located near SOHO in Hyde Park, near the Village, Downtown, Airport, and many trendy restaurants. This central location provides easy access to all the best that Tampa Bay has to offer, making it a perfect choice for those who want to enjoy all the city has to offer.
