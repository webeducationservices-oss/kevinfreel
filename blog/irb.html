Two Exceptional Indian Rocks Beach Opportunities

If you have been waiting for the right opportunity in Indian Rocks Beach, this is one of those moments worth paying attention to. It is not often that two very different but equally exciting properties become available at the same time in such a desirable beach community.

One offers the kind of waterfront lifestyle buyers dream about, with a dock, boat lifts, and the flexibility to enjoy it now or build something even bigger in the future. The other is a ready-to-go multi-family income property just one block from the beach, offering the kind of rental potential that gets investors interested fast. Whether you are looking for your own beach escape, a redevelopment opportunity, or a smart addition to your portfolio, these two properties deserve a closer look.

A Waterfront Home With Flexibility, Lifestyle, and Future Potential

419 12th Ave, Indian Rocks Beach

Located at 419 12th Ave in Indian Rocks Beach, this remodeled block home checks a lot of boxes for buyers who want waterfront access and options. The home features 2 bedrooms, 2 full baths, and a 1-car garage, all set on a 52' x 120' lot. It is the kind of property that works for different types of buyers because it can serve so many purposes.

The waterfront setup is a standout feature. With 52 feet of seawall, a dock, and two lifts, this property is built for enjoying life on the water. One lift is rated for a 28-foot boat at 13,000 pounds, and the second is a 3,000-pound lift for a jet ski. For boaters and anyone who loves having direct water access, that is a major advantage.

Inside, the home has already been updated with features that make it move-in ready and comfortable. Highlights include:

  • 2 bedrooms and 2 full baths

  • Remodeled block construction

  • New kitchen

  • New electric fireplace

  • Utility room

  • 1-car garage

What makes this property especially appealing is its flexibility. You could use it as a personal beach getaway, explore it as an income-producing Airbnb, or take advantage of the lot and waterfront setting to build your dream home in Indian Rocks Beach. It is rare to find a property that offers lifestyle value today and future upside tomorrow.

There is also an open house this Saturday from 2 to 4 p.m., giving buyers a chance to experience the location, layout, and waterfront features in person.

A Multi-Family Investment Opportunity Just One Block From the Beach

722-724 1st Street, Indian Rocks Beach

The second opportunity is ideal for investors looking for location, rental flexibility, and an already-improved property. Located at 722-724 1st Street in Indian Rocks Beach, this offering includes six remodeled 2-bedroom, 1-bath units, each approximately 800 square feet.

For beach investors, this is the kind of property that stands out because much of the hard work has already been done. The units are remodeled, updated, and ready to go, making it easier to step into ownership with income potential from day one.

Some of the key features include:

  • 6 remodeled 2-bedroom, 1-bath units

  • Approximately 800 square feet per unit

  • New windows, tile floors, kitchens, and baths

  • Two laundry rooms on site with storage

  • 8 tandem parking spaces

  • Only one block from the beach

This property also has the kind of beachside character renters look for. The updated interiors, walkable location, and laid-back coastal feel all contribute to its appeal. With daily sunsets right around the corner, the setting itself becomes part of the value.

Another major draw is the rental flexibility. Seasonal, short-term, and long-term renting are all allowed, giving investors multiple ways to approach the property based on their goals and market conditions. That kind of versatility is especially valuable in a beach community where both visitor demand and long-term desirability remain strong.

Why Indian Rocks Beach Continues to Draw Buyers and Investors

Indian Rocks Beach has long been one of those special places on the Gulf Coast that people fall in love with quickly. It offers a more relaxed and residential beach-town feel while still giving owners access to everything that makes coastal Florida living so desirable.

For homeowners, it is about the lifestyle. Waterfront access, walkability, nearby dining, and sunset views all make Indian Rocks Beach a place people want to spend time. For investors, it is about location and demand. Properties close to the water and close to the beach tend to hold strong appeal, especially when they offer flexibility in how they can be used.

These two listings represent two very different ways to own in Indian Rocks Beach. One is a waterfront home with personal-use appeal and redevelopment potential. The other is a multi-family property with strong rental and investment appeal. That is what makes this such an exciting moment.

Which Opportunity Fits You Best?

Some buyers walk into a waterfront home and immediately start picturing mornings on the dock, sunset cruises, and weekends by the water. Others look at a six-unit property one block from the beach and see numbers, occupancy, and long-term upside. The beauty of these two listings is that they speak to different goals while sharing the same powerful location advantage.

If you have been thinking about buying in Indian Rocks Beach, this is a great time to take a serious look. Opportunities like these tend to get attention quickly because they are not just properties. They are options. They are lifestyle plays. They are investment plays. And in the right hands, they can become something even more valuable over time.
