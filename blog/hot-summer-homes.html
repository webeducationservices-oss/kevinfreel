The Hottest Homes for Sale in Tampa This Summer

Watch the video →

Summer is one of the busiest times of year for homebuyers in Tampa—and for good reason. The kids are out of school, the weather is bright, and it’s the perfect window to explore neighborhoods and close before the holidays hit. But here's the catch: great homes move quickly. Especially in sought-after areas like North Hyde Park, Tampa Heights, Westshore Marina District, and Port Tampa, listings are snapped up fast—sometimes before they even hit the open market.

That’s why we put together this quick roundup of some of the most exciting homes for sale in Tampa right now, from affordable condos to brand-new luxury townhomes. If you’re a serious buyer—or thinking about becoming one—these listings are worth your attention.

Tampa Heights Real Estate: Modern Style in a Walkable District

Listing: 418 W Frances Ave, Unit #2
Price: $1.129M
Open House: Sunday, 1–3 PM
Builder: Momentum Homes

Tampa Heights continues to evolve as one of Tampa’s most walkable, culture-forward neighborhoods. And this brand-new townhome at 418 W Frances Ave is a standout example of modern living done right. With over 2,700 square feet, 4 spacious bedrooms, 3.5 bathrooms, and an oversized two-car garage, it’s built for convenience and style.

Even better, this home doesn’t require flood insurance, and it’s just minutes from Downtown Tampa, Armature Works, and Riverwalk. You’ll enjoy the perks of city life without the typical price tag of waterfront premiums.

If you’ve been looking for a move-in-ready home in Tampa Heights with new construction quality and location advantages, this is it.

If you're searching for modern homes in the Westshore Marina District, this is one not to miss.
