My Story

With over 40 years of real estate experience, I service the entire Tampa Bay area, specializing in Odessa, Lutz, St. Petersburg, The Beaches, Clearwater, Dunedin, Palm Harbor, Safety Harbor, Countryside, and South Tampa/Hyde Park.
