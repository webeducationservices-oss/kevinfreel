Your Marketing Plan

How Kevin Freel sells Tampa Bay homes faster and for more. A complete, structured marketing strategy — refined over 40 years and 1,200+ successful sales.

Kevin Freel — 40+ Years of Experience

Working 7 days a week for my clients. Get the extra effort you deserve from a Realtor. Organizing and categorizing Kevin Freel's marketing plan for real estate listings provides a clear, structured overview of the strategies employed to ensure maximum exposure for your property. Here's exactly how the plan works:

1

Digital Marketing Strategies

MLS Listings

Your property goes straight into the MLS Realtor database — including elevation, floor plan, specs, and survey — then syndicates to Century 21, Century 21 Beggins, Realtor.com, Zillow, and Trulia within 24 hours.

Social Media Promotion

Videos and property details uploaded to every major platform: Zillow, Facebook, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, X (Twitter), Google Ads, and LinkedIn.

Featured across all of Kevin's personal accounts — two Instagram accounts (including @Kevinsellstampabay), LinkedIn, YouTube, TikTok, Google Ads, and two Facebook pages.

Professional Photography & Videography

Up to 100 professional photos in the MLS database, shot by a pro photographer, plus aerial drone footage. Updated as needed throughout the listing period.

Email Marketing

Targeted email blast to thousands of Realtors in Hillsborough and Pinellas. Over 5,500 Realtors receive a color brochure with multiple photos of your home — getting it in front of every agent who has a qualified buyer.

2

Direct Outreach

Realtor Engagement

Personal calls and emails to 25+ top Tampa Bay Realtors who specialize in selling homes like yours. They hear about your property before most agents even know it's listed.

Neighborhood Awareness

"Just Listed" jumbo color postcards mailed to your neighborhood. Your neighbors know people who want to live near them — this turns that network into buyers.

REAL LISTING

Every Listing Gets the Same Attention

Whether your home is a $400k bungalow or a $2M estate, it gets the full marketing system. Same photography, same channels, same network.

3

Physical Marketing Efforts

Broker's Open Houses

Your home is featured at Wednesday Broker's Open events — up to 3 times if needed — putting it in front of local agents with qualified buyers.

Office Caravan

The full Century 21 Beggins office team caravans your home so every agent is personally familiar with what you're offering.

Monthly Open Houses

Property featured on the office wall and listing boards at C-21 Beggins Monthly Open Houses — every month until you're under contract.

4

Communication + Commitment

Weekly Email Updates

Every week you get a detailed update — showings, open house traffic, feedback, market activity, and the next steps Kevin is taking.

Seven-Day Availability
